## ROI Templates

### HVAC / Home Services

Use Performance by default when revenue and customer value justify it.

Break-even framing:

```text
Year 1 investment / $6,500 customer LCV = customers needed to break even
```

Script:

> A home service company at this size usually needs only a handful of new customers to justify the year. The real upside is not one lead source. It is being easier to find on Google, easier for AI tools to understand, and easier for visitors to contact.

### Insurance / Financial Services

Use Growth by default unless scope requires integrations.

Break-even framing:

```text
Year 1 investment / $6,000-$12,000 policyholder LCV = clients needed to break even
```

Script:

> Insurance buyers research before they call. The goal is to make the agency the trusted option that shows up first, explains clearly, and earns the first conversation.

### Professional Services

Use Foundation or Growth depending on content need.

Break-even framing:

```text
Year 1 investment / $4,000 average new client = clients needed to break even
```

Script:

> In professional services, the entire game is trust plus timing. The proposal should show how the site creates credibility, speed, clear positioning, and AI-readable authority.

### Healthcare / Dental

Use Foundation or Growth by default.

Script:

> Patients compare credibility signals carefully: reviews, clarity, speed, and whether the practice looks current. Search visibility only matters when the page also earns trust.

### Retail / E-Commerce

Use Foundation unless content or conversion work matters.

Script:

> Retail performance is about speed, clarity, and purchase confidence. The proposal should connect load time, product clarity, and repeatable organic visibility to revenue.
