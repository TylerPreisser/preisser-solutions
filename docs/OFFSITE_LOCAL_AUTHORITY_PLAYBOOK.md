# Offsite Local Authority Playbook

Use real entity signals only. Do not create fake reviews, fake engagement, fake locations, fake sponsorships, fake press, or doorway pages.

## Legitimate Setup

- Complete and verify Google Business Profile.
- Create or claim Bing Places.
- Create or claim Apple Business Connect.
- Keep Facebook Business Page and LinkedIn Company Page accurate if used.
- Add Yelp only if it is appropriate for the category.
- Join Hays Chamber or local organizations only if actually joining.
- Add FHSU/community involvement only if real.
- Request client/vendor partner links only from real relationships.
- Publish case studies only from actual work and with permission for names, screenshots, and metrics.

## Expertise Publishing

- LinkedIn posts explaining real local marketing fixes.
- YouTube or short videos walking through audits without exposing private client data.
- Reddit or forum participation only when helpful and non-spammy.
- Resource articles on the site that answer real Hays business questions.

## Review Rules

- Ask real customers for honest feedback.
- Do not ask for five stars.
- Do not coach keywords.
- Do not incentivize.
- Do not pressure.
- Do not selectively solicit only happy customers.

## Do Not Do

- Fake traffic or CTR manipulation.
- Proxy clicks, click farms, fake map interactions, or fake GBP engagement.
- Fake office addresses or virtual-office local presence.
- Keyword stuffing in the Google Business Profile name.
- Mass-generated doorway city pages.
- Fake awards, badges, certifications, sponsorships, or press.
