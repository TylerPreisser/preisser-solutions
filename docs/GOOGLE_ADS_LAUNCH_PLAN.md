## Conversion Tracking Requirements

- Form submission event after `/api/lead` success
- Phone click event
- Booking click event if a real booking URL is added
- GBP website URL with UTM parameters
- Lead-source fields passed into CRM/Zapier

## Budget Notes

Start with tightly scoped service-intent campaigns. Do not spread budget across broad, generic marketing terms until landing pages and conversion tracking prove quality.
