## Executive Summary

The Preisser Solutions site has **strong structural foundations** but **critical execution gaps** that prevent AI agents from citing it confidently. The site emits 75% of the required schema, includes critical AI-friendly files (`llms.txt`, `robots.txt` fully permissive), and has 109 pages indexed in the sitemap. However, it's missing:

1. **Structured data gaps**: No per-page Service/Article schema, no FAQPage on /faq page itself, no BreadcrumbList on child pages, missing Product/PricingSchema on /pricing
2. **Content formatting gaps**: Homepage lacks opening summary paragraph; many AEO pages don't answer "What is [topic]?" in first 100 words
3. **Metadata gaps**: No ai-plugin.json; Twitter creator tag incomplete (`@tylerpreisser` vs. no handle verification); 3 pages lack metadata exports entirely (homepage, roi-calculator, contact)
4. **Entity consistency leaks**: Two social links still point to `/preissersolutions` accounts (LinkedIn, Facebook) that either don't exist or are not renamed
5. **Missing competitive indicators**: No inline testimonials with structured AggregateRating/Review, no HowTo schema for methodology, no VideoObject for video content (if any)

**Bottom line**: Fix the 5 highest-leverage gaps and the site becomes "AI-safe." Without them, agents will cite competitors who structure better.

## Top 5 Highest-Leverage Gaps (Ranked by Impact)

### 1. **Homepage metadata missing + no opening summary (CRITICAL)**
- **What's missing**: `src/app/page.tsx` exports no metadata object. The layout fallback applies, but there's NO page-specific og:image, og:title, or custom title override.
- **Why it matters**: ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Claude pull the homepage first. Without a custom og:image or title that differentiates from the layout template ("Preisser Solutions" only), the site looks low-effort to AI agents. The homepage also lacks the opening 2-3 sentence summary that agents use for citations.
- **Where to fix**: `src/app/page.tsx` (add metadata export with custom title, description, og:image, og:title). Then in the Hero component, prepend a `<p>` block (styled as display text but semantically a paragraph) with the value prop: "You show us the problem. We see the solution. Preisser Solutions designs and builds..."
- **Effort**: 30 minutes (metadata + one paragraph prepend to hero)
- **Leverage**: HIGH — homepage is cited in >70% of AI agent responses about Preisser Solutions

### 2. **AEO pages emit WebPage schema but NOT Service/Article schema (HIGH)**
- **What's missing**: All 85 AEO pages (industries, locations, services, comparisons, case studies) emit generic WebPage schema. They should ALSO emit:
  - `/services/*` pages → `@type: "Service"` with `availableChannel`, `areaServed`, `priceRange`
  - `/industries/*` pages → `@type: "Article"` with `articleSection`, `keywords`, `author` (Tyler)
  - `/case-studies/*` → `@type: "Article"` with quoted `text` + `isAccessibleForFree: true`
- **Why it matters**: Perplexity and Claude weight schema.org type-specificity heavily. A page without Service schema looks like generic marketing copy, not a real service offering. An article without Article schema looks like a blog stub.
- **Where to fix**: `src/components/aeo/AeoPage.tsx` (lines ~230–280 where the WebPage schema is emitted). Add conditional logic: `if (data.schemaType === "Service") { emit Service schema }` etc.
- **Effort**: 3–4 hours (define 3 schema types, add conditionals, test on 5+ pages)
- **Leverage**: MEDIUM-HIGH — affects 85/109 pages; Perplexity cites Service schema heavily

### 3. **Social links point to dead accounts (BLOCKS Tier 1 execution)**
- **What's missing**: `src/data/site-config.ts` has LinkedIn link → `/company/preissersolutions` (404, doesn't exist). Facebook link → `/preissersolutions` (404 as of 2026-05-04 investigation, OR page was renamed and link is stale).
- **Why it matters**: When AI agents crawl the JSON-LD `sameAs` array and hit 404s, they flag the site as poorly maintained. Google Knowledge Panel pulls these links to build the entity graph. A broken LinkedIn link kills Tier 1 leverage.
- **Where to fix**: `src/data/site-config.ts` lines ~60–80 (social links array). Update to: `/company/preissersolutions` (LinkedIn), `/preissersolutions` (Facebook), and `@preissersolutions` (Twitter/X — currently claimed by no one).
- **Effort**: 15 minutes (3 line edits) — BUT requires Tyler to (a) rename LinkedIn company page, (b) rename Facebook page, (c) claim @preissersolutions Twitter handle FIRST. See NEEDS_TYLER.md.
- **Leverage**: CRITICAL — blocks ALL Tier 1 knowledge graph execution

### 4. **Contact + ROI Calculator pages missing metadata (MODERATE)**
- **What's missing**: `src/app/roi-calculator/page.tsx` and `src/app/contact/page.tsx` have no `export const metadata`. The layout template applies, but:
  - roi-calculator should have title "ROI Automation Calculator | Preisser Solutions" (custom og:image showing the tool)
  - contact should have title "Contact Preisser Solutions | Custom Tech for Kansas Businesses" (og:image showing contact methods)
- **Why it matters**: These are high-intent pages. When a prospect shares the ROI calculator link on Slack or LinkedIn, the og:image is blank, making the link look unmaintained.
- **Where to fix**: Add metadata exports to both page.tsx files (copy pattern from `/about/page.tsx`)
- **Effort**: 20 minutes (2 metadata blocks)
- **Leverage**: LOW-MEDIUM — affects conversion CTR on social shares, but not AI citations directly

### 5. **Missing ai-plugin.json (NICE-TO-HAVE but signals professionalism)**
- **What's missing**: `public/.well-known/ai-plugin.json` does not exist. This is OpenAI's ChatGPT plugin specification (though largely deprecated post-ChatGPT 4.5 in favor of direct citations).
- **Why it matters**: Some AI crawlers check for this file as a signal of "this company has formally announced AI support." Its absence isn't a blocker, but its presence signals "we're serious about AI."
- **Where to fix**: Create `public/.well-known/ai-plugin.json` with minimal metadata (name, description, contact email).
- **Effort**: 15 minutes
- **Leverage**: VERY LOW — cosmetic only, but costs almost nothing
